We are a nation-wide association of college and university professionals whose primary focus is to develop and facilitate opportunities for students to create and engage with the arts.

Events

AAHE 2014 Meeting

The June 2014 AAHE working meeting occurred at Boston University. We developed an organizational structure, discussed possible partnerships with other national Higher Ed and arts organizations and made plans for recruitment of new members. The University of Pittsburgh will be hosting the 2015 Annual Meeting. The June 2016 meeting will be at the University of Virginia.

The next AAHE Annual Meeting is:

June 4-5, 2015

University of Pittsburgh


For more information, contact Annabelle Clippenger - abelle@pitt.edu

Join Us

Our programs are designed to empower the creative ideas within our diverse institutions, responding to the unique goals and opportunities of each. We meet annually for approximately two days on one of our campuses to share ideas, best practices, and resources. The AAHE welcomes new members. To enquire, please contact us.
